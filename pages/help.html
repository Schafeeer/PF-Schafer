How Do I Obtain the Receipt for My Orders?

To obtain an invoice for an online purchase, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account on the retailer’s website.
  • Navigate to the "My Orders" section.
  • Select the relevant order.
  • Select the option to download or view the invoice, often labeled as "Invoice" or "Receipt".
  • If you don't see it, check your email for an order confirmation message which includeS the invoice.
  • Alternatively, contact our customer service for assistance.
My Account

Why Haven’t I Received My Verification Code / One-Time Password (OTP) via SMS?

If you haven’t received your verification code or one-time password (OTP) via WhatsApp or SMS, it could be due to two reasons:

  1. Network or Connectivity Issues. Try the following basic troubleshooting techniques:
    • Ensure you have a 4G/LTE signal from your service provider.
    • Resend a verification code (up to 4 attempts).
    • Turn off your mobile phone, remove and reinsert your SIM card, then turn your phone back on.
    • After that, request another verification code.
  2. The Account is Linked to Another Phone Number. Check your profile information to ensure the correct phone number is linked to your Shopee account. If the problem persists, contact Shopee Customer Service via Chat with the following information ready:
    • Username;
    • Date and time the issue occurred;
    • Phone model and version (Android or iOS);
    • Service provider;
    • Last known page where the verification code was requested (e.g., during password reset, updating bank account details, etc.).
Refund Policy

    How do I request a refund of an order?

    To request the refund of your purchase, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the "My Orders" section.
  3. Select the order you want to refund.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the order details page.
  5. Click the "Request Refund" button on the left side.
  6. Fill out the refund form with the required information.
  7. Submit the form and await confirmation from customer service.
Shipping & Logistics

How does my product get to me?

Our shipping follows these steps:

  • Order Processing: We confirm the order and prepare the package for shipment.
  • Shipping Method Selection: Customers choose from various shipping options.
  • Package Handling: We pack and label the items for delivery.
  • Carrier Pickup: A shipping company collects the package from our location.
  • Tracking: Customers receive a tracking number to monitor their package's journey.
  • Delivery: The package arrives at the customer's specified address.
  • Confirmation: Upon delivery, customers may receive a confirmation email or notification.
